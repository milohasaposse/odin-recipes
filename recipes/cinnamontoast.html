Cinnamon Toast

Description

Cinnamon toast, also known as cinnamon sugar toast, is a breakfast food or snack. It is not very healthy or nutritous.

I'd make like 4 of these as soon as I got home from school.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons butter or margarine
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Toast bread slices in a toaster to desired darkness. Spread butter or margarine onto one side of each slice.
  2. Stir together sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl; sprinkle generously over hot, buttered toast.
